The Bulldogs' 2026 season came to a close Tuesday in the losers bracket — a hard-fought **3-2** loss to Imperial 12U at Holyoke, despite **out-hitting them 4-3**.

**Scoring:**
- *Top 1:* Tyndle M. singled to drive in a run. **1-0 Bulldogs.**
- *Bot 1:* Emma doubled down the left-field line. **1-1.**
- *Bot 3:* Brylee singled in a run, Kaydence walked in another. **3-1 Imperial.**

**Pitching battle:** Kortlyn C. opened in the circle and gave the Bulldogs every chance — four innings, three hits, three runs, **seven strikeouts** (and seven walks). Imperial's Leah went five strong: four hits, two runs, **eight strikeouts**, three walks. Both staffs combined for 15 K.

**Haxtun hits:** Phoenix B., #23, Palmer H., and Tyndle M. each had one. RBIs from **#23** and **Tyndle M.**

**On the basepaths:** the Bulldogs ran like the playoffs — **nine stolen bases**. Kyla W. and Tyndle each swiped multiple. Imperial answered with seven of their own.

**Defense:** zero errors as a team. Palmer H. led the field with eight chances. The defense held — three runs over five-plus innings — but the offense couldn't push enough runs across.

That ends the 2026 season for the Bulldogs at **2-9 overall** (2-7 regular season + an 0-2 tournament run). A summer of growth, two wins worth celebrating, and a roster that battled to the last out.
