Haxtun Softball 12U couldn't keep up with Yuma Outlaw 12U and fell **14-7** on Thursday at the Haxtun Baseball Field.

Yuma jumped on Haxtun in the top of the first: Emerson singled down the right-field line, Brooklyn singled to right, Kimberly walked, Swayze singled to left, Kendall walked, and Natalie walked — each crossing the plate to make it 6-0.

The Bulldogs answered with **five runs on zero hits** in the bottom of the first. Amber E. was hit by a pitch (RBI), Pyper J. walked (run), Kyla W. walked (run), Tayla M. walked (run), and #23 was hit by a pitch (RBI).

Yuma added more in the second when Emerson grounded out, Charlee singled to center, and Brooklyn singled to right — each scoring a run. In the top of the fourth they broke it open with five more runs on three hits, capped by Kimberly's **inside-the-park grand slam**.

**Pitching:** Natalie earned the win for Yuma — zero hits, one run, two innings, four strikeouts, three walks. Charlee opened the circle for Yuma: zero hits and five runs over one-third of an inning with six walks.

**Bulldogs at the plate:** six different players drove in a run — Waylyn H., #23, Tayla M., Pyper J., Kyla W., and Tyndle M. The team showed real patience, drawing **11 walks** for the game. Phoenix B., Palmer H., Kyla W., and Oakley M. each took two free passes. Phoenix stole two bases.

**Defense:** zero errors as a team. Palmer H. led the field with six chances.

Next up: at Sidney on Thursday.
