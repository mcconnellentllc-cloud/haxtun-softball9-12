Haxtun couldn't keep up with #2-seed Sedg-Co in the tournament opener and fell **11-1** Monday at Holyoke Baseball Field.

Sedg-Co got on the board in the bottom of the first when Lacey T. singled down the left-field line and the Bulldogs committed an error — two runs scored. They added two more in the bottom of the second after another Haxtun error and a Lacey single up the middle. Singles by Callie T. and Lacey, plus another error, extended the lead in the third.

**Pitching:** Callie T. earned the win for Sedg-Co — one hit, one run, four innings, **nine strikeouts**, four walks. Amber E. took the loss for Haxtun: 3.1 innings, 11 runs (six earned) on eight hits, **eight strikeouts**, five walks.

**Haxtun offense:** **#23 launched the team's only hit — a triple — and drove in the run.** She went 1-for-1 with the lone RBI. The Bulldogs also turned a double play in the field and got on base via five hit-by-pitches (Kortlyn C., Kyla W., Waylyn H., Oakley M., Delaney P.).

Sedg-Co ran wild — **17 stolen bases** as a team — and didn't commit a single error.

The Bulldogs drop to the losers bracket. **Next up: Tuesday 6/23 · 10:00 AM · Small Field** vs. the Sidney/Imperial loser.
