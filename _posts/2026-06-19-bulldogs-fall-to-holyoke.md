The Bulldogs closed out the regular season Friday at Holyoke, falling **6-3** in the rescheduled May 21 make-up game — despite **out-hitting Holyoke 4-2**.

**Hits:** Kortlyn C., #23, Amber E., and Tyndle M. each had one.

**Scoring:**
- *Top 1:* Amber E. singled to drive in a run, 1-0 Bulldogs.
- *Bottom 2:* Holyoke flipped it, scoring **five runs on two hits**. Their #3 dropped a two-RBI single. 5-1 Holyoke.

**Pitching:** Tyndle M. took the loss for Haxtun — two innings, five runs on two hits, four strikeouts, seven walks. Holyoke's #18 earned the win: 3.2 IP, four hits, two runs, seven K, six BB.

**Production:** RBIs from Amber E. and Tyndle M. — back-to-back in the order. Palmer H. led the team with two walks; the Bulldogs drew **six walks** as a group. Phoenix B. and #23 each stole multiple bases as part of an **eight-stolen-base** afternoon.

That wraps the regular season at **2-8**. Up next: League Tournament June 22-24 at Holyoke.
