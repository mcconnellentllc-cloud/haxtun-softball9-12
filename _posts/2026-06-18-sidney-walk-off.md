The Bulldogs fell to Sidney 12U **5-4** on Thursday at 627 Toledo St in Sidney — on the **final play of the game**. Sidney trailed 4-1 in the bottom of the fifth when Macey S tripled to clear the bases and put the home team ahead for good.

Haxtun actually **out-hit Sidney 5-3** but couldn't close it out.

**Hits:** Phoenix B., Kortlyn C., Amber E., Palmer H., and Tyndle M. each had one.

**Scoring:**
- *Top 1:* Tyndle M. singled to put Haxtun on the board.
- *Top 3:* Phoenix B. singled, making it 2-0.
- *Bottom 5:* Sidney plated five on one hit — Macey S.'s bases-clearing triple the dagger.

**Pitching:** Kortlyn C. started in the circle and went four innings — two hits, three runs, **10 strikeouts**, eight walks. Harper G. earned the win for Sidney out of the bullpen (two-thirds of an inning). Ashlynn P. started for Sidney: four innings, five hits, four runs (three earned), six K, three BB.

**Production:** RBIs for Phoenix B., Kortlyn C., Amber E., and Tyndle M. Palmer H. stole two bases. The team had patience at the plate again — **five walks**. The Bulldogs swiped **six bases** as a team. Clean defense, no errors; Palmer led the field with 10 chances.

Next up: at Holyoke on Friday (make-up for the rained-out 5/21).
