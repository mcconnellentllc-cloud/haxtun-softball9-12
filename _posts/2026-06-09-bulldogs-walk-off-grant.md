Haxtun Softball 12U took Tuesday's game in dramatic fashion, with a **7-6 walk-off victory over Grant 12U** at the Haxtun Baseball Field. With the game tied at six in the bottom of the fifth, **Tyndle M. singled to drive in the winning run** and seal the second win of the season.

The Bulldogs took a 6-2 lead after the bottom of the fourth — a five-run rally on four hits — but Grant fought back in the top of the fifth on a double, ground out, and single to knot it at six. Tyndle's bottom-of-the-fifth single ended it.

**Kyla W. delivered the biggest swing** of the rally in the bottom of the fourth: a **two-RBI single on an 0-2 count**. **Phoenix B. went 2-for-2** to lead the team in hits, and **Aisley F. led with two walks**. The Bulldogs racked up **eight hits**, **five walks**, and **seven stolen bases** for the game and didn't commit a single error in the field. **Palmer H. had the most chances defensively with 10**.

In the circle: **Phoenix B. started** and went four innings, giving up three runs on six hits with **nine strikeouts** and four walks. **Amber E. came in to close** and earned the win — two-thirds of an inning with one strikeout and one walk.

The Bulldogs are now 2-4 on the season. Next up: Thursday at home.
